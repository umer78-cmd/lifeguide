🏛️ Brand Kit: Life Institute
Official Design System & Brand Guidelines
1. Design Essence
The Life Institute brand is built on the concept of Institutional Sanctuary. It is a space designed to feel professional, grounded, and poetic.
Core Theme: Systemic Wisdom & Institutional Sanctuary.
Visual Vibe: Airy, premium, and rhythmic.
Interaction Style: Smooth, unhurried, scroll-triggered animations that evoke a sense of calm arrival.

3. Typography
Consistency in typography is the backbone of the brand's premium, authoritative feel.
Primary Serif: Playfair Display
Aesthetic: Elegant, classic, and authoritative.
Usage: Used for Headings (h1, h2, h3), Quotes, and Italic highlights.
Character: Subtitles must use tracking-wide (0.3em) to maintain an "unhurried" look.
Primary Sans: Inter
Aesthetic: Modern, clean, and highly readable.
Usage: Used for Body text, UI elements, and metadata.
Character: Body blocks should utilize leading-relaxed and font-light settings.
4. Signature Design Elements
To maintain the "Unhurried Path" of the user experience, the following components are mandatory:
Brush Highlights: Applied to "Awakening" words to draw intentional focus.
Staggered Reveals: Content must "lift" into place to create a sense of arrival.
The "Unhurried Path": Scroll-driven SVG paths that guide the user visually through the narrative.
Marquee Strips: Used for "Life Potential" keywords to create subtle, continuous motion.
